About The Sibol Project

The Sibol Project Philippines is dedicated to empowering individuals with autism spectrum disorders and supporting their families through comprehensive services, education, and community awareness programs.

Our Mission

To provide holistic support, education, and advocacy for individuals with autism and their families, promoting inclusion, understanding, and acceptance within our communities.

Our Vision

A society where every individual with autism is valued, supported and empowered to reach their full potential, living with dignity and participating meaningfully in their communities.
