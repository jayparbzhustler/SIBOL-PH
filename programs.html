Our Programs

Tailored programs to support individuals with autism at every stage of life.

Early Intervention Program

Designed for young children, focusing on crucial developmental milestones through play-based therapy and parent involvement.

  • Speech & Language Development
  • Social Skills Building
  • Fine & Gross Motor Skills

School Readiness Program

Preparing children for successful integration into school environments, focusing on academic and social preparedness.

  • Classroom Behavior & Routine
  • Academic Pre-skills
  • Peer Interaction Strategies

Adolescent Transition Program

Supporting teenagers in developing independence, vocational skills, and navigating social complexities.

  • Life Skills Training
  • Vocational Guidance
  • Social Competence Development

Adult Support & Integration

Empowering adults with autism to live independently, secure employment, and actively participate in community life.

  • Employment Support
  • Independent Living Skills
  • Community Engagement
